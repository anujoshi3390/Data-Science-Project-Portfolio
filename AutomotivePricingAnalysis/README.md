# Automotive Pricing Analysis

## Overview
This project examines automotive pricing data to identify key customer segments and pricing drivers. Regression and tree-based models were used to understand how different features influence vehicle prices.

## Business Question
What factors most influence vehicle pricing, and how can customers be segmented to support pricing strategy?

## Approach
- Conducted exploratory analysis on vehicle and customer attributes
- Built regression and tree-based models to capture nonlinear relationships
- Identified high-impact pricing variables
- Segmented customers based on purchasing behavior

## Tools & Techniques
- Python
- Pandas, NumPy
- Scikit-learn
- Regression models
- Tree-based models

## Key Takeaways
- Pricing is influenced by a combination of vehicle features and customer attributes
- Tree-based models captured nonlinear pricing effects
- Insights can support data-driven pricing and marketing strategies
